Print Project

The image before you is the final product of my print project. We were tasked with designing a few rough bookmarks that could be presented to an elementary school. We then revised one of our designs on Adobe Indesign. After finalizing a bookmark we mass printed them to deliver to Letort elementary. As a kid, cars and space fascinated me so I decided to create a bookmark that would appeal to younger children. Hence why I went with a space theme. 		Home
